About this Fellowship

The openEHR Fellowship 2025 includes 10 fellows divided into three streams: Leadership, Clinical Modelling, and General.

This particular fellowship is part of the Clinical Modelling stream and is mentored by Heather Leslie.

The detailed goals and expected results will be explained in the subsequent documentation. In a nutshell, the goal is to create a tool that will help clinical knowledge modellers identify existing archetypes that fulfill the needs of their prospective models, as well as identify the need for new archetypes or change requests.

The program started with the kick-off meeting on 29th of May 2025 and will run for 12 months.

You can find more information here: https://openehr.org/fellowship-program-2025/
