Initial User Questionnaire

This questionnaire is designed to gather insights from professionals involved in clinical data modeling in openEHR. It covers participants’ backgrounds, their approaches to drafting and compiling data elements, and their experiences with searching, planning, and mapping archetypes.

The survey also explores the tools and methods used for creating and validating openEHR templates, as well as challenges faced during the modeling process. We hope that the collected feedback will help guide the development of tools and processes that better support the needs of the openEHR community.

Specifically we want to use this questionnaire to confirm the necessity of the proposed project.

The questionnaire will be published at the openEHR discourse forum.
